## 1. 🏢 Executive Summary: What is Elvrona?
**Elvrona** is a high-tech holding group and the architectural parent company of the **Wakil AI** ecosystem. It specializes in the development, hosting, and strategic distribution of autonomous intelligence, neural networks, and AI-driven commercial platforms.

While individual projects focus on end-user solutions, **Elvrona** provides the unifying vision, high-performance infrastructure, and uncompromising security standards for all its ventures.
